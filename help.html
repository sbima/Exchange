Useful Tips to Acess the Site

What is this

This is Exchange of Information, Information related to Computer Science. You will have an oppurtunity to Exchange your knowledge with other people around the world and also can gain some. All you have to do is to login to this site and you are free to ask your doubts and also clarify others doubts.

Why to use it

Although you have plenty of resources to learn or practice programming, But you will not be able to find any source for bugs or errors while coding, you will need someone to help you overcome that situation. "Exchange" is a platform where in you can ask your queries and people around the world can answers your question and also can share some extra informantion related to your query, You can answer some of the questions asked by others and help them overcome that situation

Overview

The Home button (onto right) will show all the Top questions asked by various people. These top questions are based upon the votes every person gives if they like the question, a question with highest votes is the top question(which is most recommended to see
You need to register into this site before hand so that you get to answer any question, all you need to do is to just click on the "Signup"(top right of the page) tab and you have to fill the form with appropriate details so as to create your identity for asking or answering the questions Once you register, you have to login into the site and you are free now to surf the questions and ask your queries over there. To answer any question all you need to do is to click on the particular question and start your answer in the box below the question you can upvote/downvote any question based upon your like/dislike to that question.

After Login

As soon as you login you should see your login name in the place of login button which indicates that you are loggedin, if you click on that tab you can see your personalized page where you can add a picture of yours which will be display picture. You can also search for a particular question in search bar, instead of seaching a question of a particular type everywhere in the questions you can directly search for the user who wrote that question or can give a keyword to find that question.
Also you can ask a question by clicking on "Ask Question" tab and see all question under "All Questions" tab where you can not only see the top questions but also the questions which are asked by every user in "Exchange". The "My Questions" tab consits of question that you have asked previously, on clicking it you can see how many users answerd your question and also you can mark an answer, by doing that it is correct answer of all the answers(or the working method of your asked question)

Other useful Information

You can see the number of votes and the number of answers (to a question) on the left side of each question and the user name who asked that question on the rightside of that question. By clicking on a user you can see his/her account details.
